Kiriko

Kiriko is a support character in a game namely Overwatch. Her utilities enhances the teams durability and speed. She can teleport to any teammate and heal them while her ultimate gives the team speed and strength boost.




Genji

Genji is a DPS character in a game namely Overwatch. Genji has shurikanes wherein you can either burst or spread. Genji can double jump, wall climb and dash onto the enemy. His ultimate allows him to reset the cooldown of his dash whenever he kills an enemy. He also has an ability where he can deflect projectiles.
